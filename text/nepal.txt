Nepal is a landlocked country located in South Asia, between China and India.
The country is home to the highest mountain in the world, Mount Everest, which stands at an elevation of 8,848 meters (29,029 feet).
Nepal has a diverse range of landscapes, including the Himalayan mountain range, forests, valleys, and plains.
Nepali is the official language of Nepal, but many other languages are spoken in the country, including Hindi and English.
Nepal is a predominantly Hindu country, but it also has a significant Buddhist population. The country has a rich cultural heritage, with many temples, monasteries, and other historical sites.
